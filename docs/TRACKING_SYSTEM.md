# Tracking System

Principle:
Use one landing page and unique URL parameters.

Do not create separate landing pages for every Facebook group.

Example:
https://kromiuniverse.com/arc?group=fb_001

The group parameter must be captured and passed into:
- hidden form field
- Kit custom field
- GA4 reports
- CRM daily reports

Metrics to track:
- visitors
- ARC signups
- Amazon clicks
- reviews
- conversion rates
- outcome score

Do not track reach or impressions.
